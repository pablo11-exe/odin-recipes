LASAGNAAA

An italian masterpiece

Pasta, tomatoes, ground beef and cheese, what else do you want?

INGREDIENTS

  • Pasta
  • Tomatoes sauce
  • Grounded beef
  • Cheese

STEPS

  1. Boil the sauce with the grounded beef
  2. Set the pasta like a tower
  3. Put sauce and cheese between
  4. To the oven for 20 minutes
